## Overview

Avians are the most adaptable aerial species, defined not by insect-like constant wingbeat but by true powered flight, visual acuity, and comfort in both land and air environments.

## Content

<details>
    <summary>Text-only display of species talents.</summary>
    <b>Stats:</b>
    <ul>
        <li><b>Constitution:</b> 3</li>
        <li><b>Endurance:</b> 4</li>
        <li><b>Effervescence:</b> 5</li>
        <li><b>Gifted:</b> Speed, Awareness, Appeal</li>
        <li><b>Block Rating:</b> 1d4</li>
        <li><b>Dodge Rating:</b> 3d12</li>
        <li><b>Size:</b> Small or Medium</li>
    </ul>
    <b>Quirks:</b>
    <ul>
        <li>You have 3 meters of Air travel, at the cost of 3 Stamina.</li>
        <li>You have favor on Awareness tests that rely on sight.</li>
    </ul>
    <b>Tree 1:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Raptor Dive:</b> When you move at least 4 meters before a melee attack, deal +1d6 Physical. (1/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Wingguard Roll:</b> While flying, you gain +2 Physical Defense. (1/0/1)</li>
        <li><b>Talon Rend:</b> Your weaponless attacks deal 2d8 Physical damage instead of 1d6. (1/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Sky Execution:</b> When attacking a creature below your elevation, deal +2d10 Physical damage. (2/0/1)</li>
    </ol>
    <b>Tree 2:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Mimic Call:</b> You may perfectly imitate any sound you have heard in the last day. (0/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Decoy Call:</b> You may spend 3 stamina to create an audio distraction in a point within 15m. Creatures must pass an Awareness test (TD = 10 + your Language mod) or treat the sound as a valid threat for 1 round. (0/1/1)</li>
        <li><b>Warbird's Cry:</b> As a turn action, you may spend 4 stamina to mark a creature you can see. Until the start of your next turn, allies gain +1d6 damage against that creature. (0/2/0)</li>
        <li><b>Flock Execution:</b> When a creature marked by your Warbird's Cry is damaged by an ally, you may spend 2 stamina (no action) to deal +1d6 Physical damage to the same target. (0/2/1)</li>
    </ol>
    <b>Tree 3:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Adept Flier:</b> You gain +1d12 Dodge rating. (2/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Cooperative Instinct:</b> When an ally within 5m succeeds on a test, you may spend 2 stamina to gain Favor on your next test. (0/1/1)</li>
        <li><b>Soaring Spirit:</b> When you take the Deep Breath action while outdoors, you regain 1d4 stamina. (0/0/3)</li>
    </ol>
</details>
