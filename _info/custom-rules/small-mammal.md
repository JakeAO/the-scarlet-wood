## Overview

Small mammals are lightweight, fast-moving, and highly alert creatures such as mice, rats, squirrels, gophers, shrews, and similar species. Their survival is based on mobility, instinct, and rapid response to danger rather than raw strength or endurance.

    </ol>
    <b>Tree 2:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Burrower's Start:</b> You can Burrow at half your Speed through loose soil or sand. (1/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Scramble Climber:</b> You gain +2 meters when climbing and no longer move at half speed while doing so. You gain a point of favor on Scale tests. (2/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Narrow Escape:</b> When you succeed on a Dodge test by 5 or more, you may immediately move 2 meter without spending movement. (1/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Uncatchable:</b> You gain a point of favor on tests to avoid being grabbed, grappled, or restrained. (0/2/0)</li>

    <b>Tree 3:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Adrenal Twitch:</b> When you drop below half SHP, gain +1d12 Dodge for the rest of the encounter. (1/0/0)</li>

        <li><b>Survival Priority:</b> When you would drop to 0 SHP, drop to 1 SHP instead and immediately move 2 meters (1/rest). (2/0/1)</li>
