## Overview

Medium mammals represent the broadest and most adaptable group in the forest ecosystem. They are neither as physically overwhelming as large mammals nor as evasive and fragile as small mammals. Instead, they specialize in versatility â€” able to shift between scouting, combat, problem-solving, tool use, and environmental interaction depending on need.

    <ol>
        <li><b>Read the Opening:</b> You have favor on Block and Dodge checks against creatures who have attacked you before that turn. (0/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Cooperative Gift:</b> When an ally is within 2 meters of your target, your melee attacks deal +1d6 damage. (0/0/2)</li>
        <li><b>Exploit Weakness:</b> When you hit a creature you have damaged before, you may spend 3 stamina to deal +2d8 Physical damage. (2/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Flow of Battle:</b> After making a melee attack, you may either gain +1d12 Dodge or +1d4 Block until start of next turn. (2/1/0)</li>
    </ol>
    <b>Tree 2:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Forager's Gift:</b> You gain an additional die of material when foraging. (0/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Natural Engineer:</b> You gain a point of favor when interacting with simple machines, mechanisms, and objects. (1/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Field Repair:</b> You may spend 5 stamina and 1 minute to restore a broken item, structure, or vehicle. (0/2/0)</li>
        <li><b>Rapid Improviser:</b> You can spend 10 stamina and one turn action to create a single crude or common consumable item without using any material components. (0/3/0)</li>
    </ol>
    <b>Tree 3:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Instinctive Shift:</b> Once per encounter, you gain favor on a test of your choice. (0/1/0)</li>

        <li><b>Prodigy:</b> You choose one subtrait to become gifted in. (0/3/0)</li>
