## Overview

Crawling insects are resilient, adaptable, and specialized. They are defined by exoskeletal protection, multi-limbed efficiency, and environmental mastery.

## Content

<details>
    <summary>Text-only display of species talents.</summary>
    <b>Stats:</b>
    <ul>
        <li><b>Constitution:</b> 5</li>
        <li><b>Endurance:</b> 5</li>
        <li><b>Effervescence:</b> 2</li>
        <li><b>Gifted:</b> 2 subtraits of your choice</li>
        <li><b>Block Rating:</b> 3d4</li>
        <li><b>Dodge Rating:</b> 1d12</li>
        <li><b>Size:</b> Small</li>
    </ul>
    <b>Quirks:</b>
    <ul>
        <li>You have 2 extra hands.</li>
        <li>You cannot wear head or body armor.</li>
        <li>Each of your defenses is increased by 2.</li>
    </ul>
    <b>Tree 1:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Chitin Serration:</b> Your weaponless attacks deal 2d6 Physical instead of 1d6. (1/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Multi-Limb Strikes:</b> Once per turn, when you make an attack, you may spend 2 stamina to make a weaponless attack for free. (1/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Venomous Edge:</b> Once per attack, you may spend 3 stamina to deal +2d8 Heat or Psyche damage. (2/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Exoskeleton Breaker:</b> When you deal damage to a creature, you may spend 2 stamina to deal bonus damage equal to the target's defense against that attack's damage type. (1/2/0)</li>
    </ol>
    <b>Tree 2:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Colony Builder:</b> You gain favor on Engineering tests related to creating, destroying, or modifying structures and buildings. (0/1/0)</li>
        <li><b>Pheromone Sense:</b> You may spend 1 turn action to learn the nearest path taken by a creature who passed through the area within the last hour. (0/1/1)</li>
        <li><b>Efficient Burrower:</b> You may spend 5 stamina to excavate 1 cubic meter of dirt, sand, or soft material as a turn action. The excavated soil acts as an obstacle for the purposes of Take Cover. (2/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Hive Protector:</b> When an ally within 6 meters of you makes a dodge or block check, you can spend an interrupt action and 4 stamina to give them a point of favor. (0/1/2)</li>
    </ol>
    <b>Tree 3:</b>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Rigid Carapace:</b> You gain +2 Physical defense. (1/0/0)</li>
        <li><b>Alien Mind:</b> You gain +4 Psyche defense. (0/2/0)</li>
        <li><b>Regenerative Molting:</b> When you take the Deep Breath action, regain 1d6 SHP. (1/0/1)</li>
    </ol>
</details>
